Network Segmentation

Properly segmenting your home network is crucial. I created a secure subnet for cameras, isolating them from your main devices to reduce the risk of intrusion or data theft.

Figure 1: Home Network Segmentation Layout

  • Create a separate Guest network or VLAN for IoT devices.
  • Use strong Wi-Fi encryption (WPA3).
  • Update router firmware regularly to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Implement firewall rules to restrict camera access.

Wi-Fi Selection

Selecting the right Wi-Fi frequency is essential to ensure cameras stay online. After evaluating your router’s bandwidth, I chose the 2.4GHz range for its better range through walls, ensuring consistent connectivity across your home.

Frequency Range Speed Interference
2.4GHz Longer range, better penetration through walls. Moderate speed. Higher interference from devices like microwaves.
5GHz Shorter range, less penetration through walls. Higher speed. Lower interference, more channels available.
  • Enhanced coverage ensures all cameras remain connected.
  • Minimized interference improves signal stability.
  • Optimized channel selection reduces network congestion.

Troubleshooting

Encountered issues with viewpoint, connectivity, or notifications? Here are some typical steps I take to resolve common problems:

  1. Check signal strength and reposition the camera or add a Wi-Fi extender.
  2. If the camera angle is limited, install a wedge or bracket for better coverage.
  3. Re-run network setup if the camera is offline or unresponsive.
  4. Review camera logs for error codes or conflicting IP addresses.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access camera footage from a while back?

You can access archived footage through the cloud storage feature in the app. Simply navigate to the playback section and select the desired date and time.

What should I do if my camera is not responding?

First, ensure the camera is properly connected to the network. If the issue persists, try restarting the camera and your router. If problems continue, contact support for further assistance.
