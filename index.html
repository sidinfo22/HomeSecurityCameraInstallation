Network Segmentation

Properly segmenting your home network is crucial. We create a secure subnet for cameras so they are isolated from your main devices. This reduces risk of intrusion or data theft.

  • Create separate Guest network or VLAN for IoT devices
  • Use strong Wi-Fi encryption (WPA2/WPA3)
  • Update router firmware regularly

Wi-Fi Selection

We evaluate your router’s bandwidth and choose the right frequency to ensure cameras stay online. Often 2.4GHz has better range through walls, while 5GHz provides faster speed but shorter range.

Our approach:

  1. Check 2.4GHz signals for coverage throughout the house
  2. Balance speed vs. distance for optimal camera streaming
  3. Minimize interference from other wireless networks

App Integration

Troubleshooting

Encountered issues with viewpoint, connectivity, or notifications? Here are some typical steps we take:

  1. Check signal strength and reposition the camera or add a Wi-Fi extender.
  2. If camera angle is limited, install a wedge or bracket for better coverage.
  3. Re-run network setup if camera is offline or unresponsive.
  4. Review camera logs for error codes or conflicting IP addresses.

Often, small adjustments in mounting location, wedge installation, or advanced bracket positioning can vastly improve coverage and clarity.
